**Strategy Summary:**
The "Sell in May, Buy in October" strategy is a seasonal investing approach that suggests selling stocks on the last trading day of May and repurchasing them on the first trading day of October. Historically, this strategy aims to avoid the underperformance observed in the stock market during the summer months and capitalize on stronger performance in the fall and winter.

### Used code for buy/sell signals
```python
import pandas as pd
import os   

def generate_signals():
    file_input_path = os.path.join('path_to_your_data', 'stock_data.csv')
    df = pd.read_csv(file_input_path)

    df['Date'] = pd.to_datetime(df['Date'])
    df.set_index('Date', inplace=True)

    # Sell on the last working day of May
    df['SellSignal'] = (df.index.month == 5) & (df.index.isin(df.resample('BM').last().index))
    
    # Buy on the first working day of October
    df['BuySignal'] = (df.index.month == 10) & (df.index.isin(df.resample('B').first().index))

    df['BuySignal'] = df['BuySignal'].fillna(False)
    df['SellSignal'] = df['SellSignal'].fillna(False)

    df_output = df[['BuySignal', 'SellSignal']]
    df_output.to_csv('stock_signals.csv', index=False)

generate_signals()
```

# multi-agent-workflow.py

## Input

Research MSFT's current opportunity and key risks.

## Output

### Expected response

```markdown
## MSFT Research Brief

### Opportunity to investigate
Review Azure growth, Microsoft 365 and security recurring revenue, AI product adoption, operating margins, free-cash-flow conversion, and the return on AI infrastructure investment using dated public filings and earnings materials.

### Key risks
- AI monetization may lag data-center, GPU, power, and depreciation costs.
- Cloud competition, enterprise-spending pressure, regulation, cybersecurity incidents, and high valuation expectations can affect results and share-price volatility.

### Limitations
This workflow supplies a research framework, not current market data, price targets, or a transaction recommendation. Conclusions require validation against the latest company disclosures and independent sources.
```

The workflow passes market context through risk review to the brief writer before emitting the final response.

# harness-agent.py

## Input

Build a research plan for comparing two broad-market ETFs.

## Output

### Expected response

```markdown
## Broad-Market ETF Comparison Plan

1. Confirm each fund's legal name, benchmark, investment universe, domicile, and share class.
2. Compare holdings count, top-ten concentration, sector and country exposure, market-cap profile, and index-rebalancing rules.
3. Compare expense ratio, tracking difference, assets under management, trading volume, bid-ask spread, and NAV premium or discount using one as-of date.
4. Review long-horizon performance, drawdowns, volatility, distributions, tax treatment, and portfolio overlap.
5. Document market, concentration, liquidity, currency, methodology, and issuer risks, plus missing evidence.

The comparison requires the two ETF tickers and the investor's time horizon, account type, and intended portfolio role before fund-specific conclusions can be made. This is research only, not investment advice.
```
